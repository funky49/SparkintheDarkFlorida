Outreach & Programs

basslab.science

Focus: Acoustics, Physics, and Fluid Dynamics

An interactive educational experience that bridges technology and physical science. Using a custom web application, basslab generates specific low-frequency sounds designed to interact visually and physically with non-Newtonian fluids (like oobleck). This provides a striking, hands-on demonstration of cymatics and sound wave energy.

About

We are dedicated to noncommercial research and the dissemination of scientific information for public education and general welfare.

Hello from Steve Rush! I am the founder of Spark in the Dark Florida. My background is rooted in the intersection of technology, music, and science.

I started this organization because I believe scientific literacy is crucial for the general welfare of the public. By translating complex ideas into interactive, understandable experiences, we can spark curiosity and a lifelong love of learning.

For years I have used hip-hop as a tool for science outreach. I have rapped for the National Fermi Accelerator Laborator, The Planetary Society, the National Science Foundation, the USA Science & Engineering Festival, and the St. Petersburg Science Festival. I have also performed at Nerdapalooza and the Orlando Nerd Fest.

Spark in the Dark Florida is the culmination of several of my interests.

Vision: Publish software to help science teachers and communicators. Create new exhbits for science museum and festivals. Ally with science outreach organizations.
